About Jabong:


Jabong.com is an Indian Online fashion & Apparel store funded by Germany based incubator Rocket Internet. It specializes in fashion, apparel but also deals in furniture and homewares. The store at present has over 200 brands and 20,000 products. Tagged as "Delivering lifestyle", Jabong.com was founded in 2011 by Praveen Sinha, Arun Chandra Mohan and Lakshmi Potluri. The company is presently working in Indian demographic. Within 4 months of launch of operations, the company has over a million page views daily with more than 160,000 unique visitors
